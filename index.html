Hello there! Welcome to my site
and let me introduce myself...

I'm a student of computer science at UCM in Spain. For ages I have been a videogames lover and, whenever I have some free time, I spend it on develop a game-idea or fill my head with new ones by gaming. Still, I do not only focus on videogames, since I am also quite interested in parallel programming and machine learning or A.I.

During my third year, I won the bronze medal in a nationwide parallel programing contest in pairs in which I had to participate by myself representing my University (link to the contest results.).

Thanks to the abilities and capacity I demonstrate in the contest, a professor of the Computing Department of my University took me as an intern to collaborate in projects related with parallel programming and library porting while I was finishing my studies.

But development is not all I am interested about... Art, painting and music are also things I am focus on. Modeling of characters, maps and levels or sound tracks for videogames are just some of the things I love to do in my freetime.

I am currently working in various projects related with the thing listed above, but why don't you take a look yourself? Below I list my projects that can be found in Github and Pinterest as well.
